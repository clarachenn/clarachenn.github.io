about me

Hello, I am Clara!

I am a second-year Computer Science student at the University of California, Irvine. My journey in software development began in 9th grade, and since then, I have cultivated a passion for all things related to technology. As I progress through my studies, I am eager to explore advanced topics, engage in meaningful projects, and continue my growth in the dynamic field of computer science.

Furthermore, my commitment extends to projects, and I proudly contribute as a Web Developer for CanSat, combining my technical skills with a passion for innovation. Beyond the realm of technology, I am also a member of the Taekwondo Club at UCI.
