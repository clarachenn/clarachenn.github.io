about me

Hello! I am Clara.

I am a second-year Computer Science student at the University of California, Irvine. My journey in software development began in 9th grade, and since then, I have cultivated a passion for all things related to technology. I am proficient in languages such as Python, Java, C++, HTML, and CSS. As I progress through my studies, I am eager to explore advanced topics, engage in meaningful projects, and continue my growth in the dynamic field of computer science.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively engage in a diverse range of extracurricular activities both on and off campus. I serve as a dedicated Academic and Alumni Committee Member at the ICS Student Council, contribute as a Corporate Committee Member for VenusHacks, and participate in Hack at UCI. Furthermore, my commitment extends to projects, and I proudly contribute as a Web Developer for CanSat, combining my technical skills with a passion for innovation. Beyond the realm of technology, I am also a member of the Taekwondo Club at UCI.
